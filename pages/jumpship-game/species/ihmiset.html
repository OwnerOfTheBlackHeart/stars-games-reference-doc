Appearance and Biology

The ihmiset are a fur-covered, humanoid species that average 0.5 meters (1' 6") in height with dragon-like eyes, a mouth full of sharp teeth, a single, large talon on each of their hands and feet, blunt digging claws, and a tail nearly as long as they are. Female ihmiset have bone plates and spikes along their backs, dull colored fur that helps them blend in, and stand around 0.2 meters (6 inches) taller than males. Meanwhile, males have dark, rich furs typically of green, red, or blue color, colorful frills along their back, and a crown of horns designed to lock into the horns of other males.

Despite their appearance suggesting obligate predation, ihmiset are actually omnivores. Their natural diet is a mix of leathery skinned fruits, large insects, small lizards and mammals, and megafauna that tribes of ihmiset attack in large groups that use their sharp teeth to rip open the skin of the beasts to cause blood loss over long hunts.

Ihmiset have the ability to spray a sticky, smelly irritant as means of self defense. While one ihmiset's ejection is unpleasant, the ihmiset prefer to spray threats in groups, creating overwhelming itchiness and pain and a smell that would make a skunk turn their nose up.

It is common for ihmiset to get the assisted glide system cyberware.

Government, Structure, and Society

The ihmiset organize themselves into tribes of 100 to 300 hundred members based on family that travel together in subgroups of 5 to 20 ihmisets. For proper governance, provinces elect their leaders for both the province and planetary positions. Federal/imperial positions, on the other hand, are elected only by chieftains or elders appointed by a chieftain for the purpose of the vote. All elected positions have to cater to the chieftains as most individuals in any given tribe will vote for the same officials as their chieftain.

Due to the ihmisets' love of exploring, those ihmisets that are not present for a vote either leave their choices with a relative to vote for them or accept that their vote will automatically match that of their chieftain.

Technology

The ihmiset use mostly standard technology, with their specialty being in spacecraft, terraforming, and vehicles.

  • Spikeships
    • Ihmiset built spikeships can travel 1 extra hex per jump
    • Ihmiset built spikeships have only half the required crew
  • System Ships
    • Ihmiset built system ships have +1 speed
    • Ihmiset built system ships produce require only 1 fuel point per 2 speed the system ship chooses to travel at, rounding down
    • Ihmiset built system ships have doubled maximum crew
    • Ihmiset built system ships have artificial gravity for free
    • Ihmiset built system ships of frigate class, or smaller, have atmospheric operation for free
    • Ihmiset built system ships of cruiser class have atmospheric landing for free, even though they cannot normally mount the fitting
    • Hydroponic Garden
      • Ihmiset hydroponic gardens produce twice as many supplies as normal
    • Extended Life Support
      • Ihmiset built extended life support fittings take up 0.5# power and 0.5# mass, rounding up
  • Armor
    • Ihmiset built armor with the sealed quality reduces the armor's encumbrance by 1 (minimum 0)
    • Ihmiset built armor with the sealed quality does not suffer a risk of tearing except from dedicated suit rippers
