Appearance and Biology

The abantu are beetle-like humanoids standing roughly 3 meters (10 feet) tall with sturdy, thick chitinous shells, short mandibles that hug tightly to the mouth, short stiff antenna, large, complex eyes, and four arms. Abantu shells are colorful and metallic, the exact color determined by the diet of the abantu, with many abantu eating various ores or pearls to develop their preferred shell colors. Abantu hands possess three fingers and a thumb. The abantu lack any means to fly and have a solid back plate.

The abantu are herbivorous with a preference for woody plants and bark, though they will scavenge fresh kills given the chance.

Government, Structure, and Society

The abantu like to keep their government efficient and transparent, usually having government officials voted in to act as representatives present proposals to the collective people the proposal will effect, usually the county or state the proposal affects, who then vote on whether to accept or deny the proposal. At the planetary or imperial level, positions are elected and don't have to go through getting votes from the whole of the affected populace, however any decision they make can be overturned by collective vote and if they preform poorly, they may not see their next term in office.

Technology

The abantu favor innovative and advanced technologies over simple and tested technologies, and prefer to protect themselves with armor as thick and heavy as their own shells.

  • System Ships

    • Abantu made energy weapons increase their magazine value by 50% rounding up

  • Plasma Weapons

    • Abantu made plasma weapons gain +2 extra damage
