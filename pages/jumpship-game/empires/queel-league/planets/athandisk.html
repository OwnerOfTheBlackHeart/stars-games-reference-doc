The planet of Athandisk is a warm world with an equatorial desert and habitable norther and southern latitudes with a breathable atmosphere. Lacking any native life, pockets of livable land have been created thanks to prior terraformation efforts seeding different locations with plants and animals native to Melis. Unfortunately, the terraformer caused the planet to become too hot after the machine failed, but its failure has left the planet with several strange minerals that, aside from not forming naturally and being near impossible to synthesize, are little more than odd ores for well known materials. These minerals, however, create a unique soil environment that allows the growing of Aathire, a fruiting bush unique to Athandisk, whose fruits are used in the creation of several highly sought after drugs within the Queel League.

The people of Athandisk are stuck living simple, low tech lives as the megacorporations that control the mining and Aathire growing operations horde all the unprocurable technologies for themselves.
