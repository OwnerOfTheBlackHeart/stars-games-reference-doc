Queel League

  • Primary Species: Ihmiset
  • Primary Currency: Rahaa
  • NPCs

The Queel League is a league of planetary governments formed during the ihmisets' period of expansion and colonization. The largest planet in the League is the homeworld of the ihmiset, Melis. Most of the colonies of the League are either resource extraction or scientific study outposts, only Athandisk being old and valuable enough to maintain a large, permanent population.

The Queel League maintains good relations with the Wernume Imperium, who the ihmisets find amusing and interesting. The opposite is true of the Nox'paj Alliance, which the League views as an untrustworthy neighbor, who has made several attempts at destabilizing the League on multiple occasions. The fact that ijudi are as selfish and disloyal as they are do not help the ihmisets' opinion of the Nox'paj Alliance.
