Technology

Maximoffian technology is designed to be energy efficient and to resist the elements while traveling off the beaten path or being used on extended patrols.

  • Energy Weapons
    • Maximoffian made energy weapons have 50% extra magazine size, rounding up
  • Powered Armor
    • Maximoffian made powered armor has +1 AC
    • Maximoffian made powered armor lasts for 1 week per B-Cell
  • Melee Weapons
    • Maximoffian made melee weapons require 14 strength to use or apply a -4 penalty to attack rolls
    • Maximoffian made melee weapons increase the damage die by 1 stage
  • Equipment
    • Maximoffian made equipment that uses up electricity lasts twice as long per A-Cell, B-Cell, or battery
    • Solar Recharger
      • Maximoffian made solar rechargers can charge up to 4 A-Cells at once or 1 B-Cell over 48 hours
    • Telekinetic Generator
      • Maximoffian made telekinetic generators weigh only 1 encumbrance
    • Maximoffians can produce the unique item "surfboard" for 10 credits given a sufficient supply of light wood, or sturdy foam, and sealing wax
