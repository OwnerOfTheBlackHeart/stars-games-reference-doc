Williams 6

  • NPCs
  • Occupation: Deranged but brilliant scientist
  • Situation: Vault for dangerous pretech

Williams 6 is a deep space station built from an old factory ship with 2 mobile shipyards from the original Maximoffian Empire fleet. A large portion of lisaten have made the Williams home, and many more routinely visit the station for fuel and repairs.

The lisaten that call the Williams home have a poor opinion of the Maximoffian Enclave due to the Enclave's restrictive laws, something the naturally chaotic lisaten find deeply frustrating.
