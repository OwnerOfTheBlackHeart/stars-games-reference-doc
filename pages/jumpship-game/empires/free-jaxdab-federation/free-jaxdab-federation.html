Free Jaxdab Federation

  • Primary Species: Human
  • Primary Currency: Mandate Credit
  • NPCs

The Free Jaxdab Federation, ruled from it's capital on Thurid, is a loose federation of planets that are "willingly" united in trade and space defense. Thurid remains the most influential of the planets in the Federation thanks to its technological advantage and higher population, which the local Exchange Consulate isn't afraid to use deniable operations to ensure it maintains.

The Free Jaxdab Federation generally ignores the Peoples' Republic of Salmy as the nearest planet, Ambee, isn't valuable enough to draw the attention of Thurid. Meanwhile, the Nox'paj Alliance is viewed as a threat and their planet of Lorne Maie is viewed as a desired prize by Thurid.

Nobility from Thurid use mechs as a symbol of status, one which only nobles have access to, save those who have adequately impressed a noble of high enough status. While this quirk would normally be of little effect to most, thuridian nobles are common alongside Exchange Consulate diplomats in helping to maintain Thurid's supremacy and monitor the lesser planets and nations within the Federation. For historical and traditional reasons, it is rare for male nobles to own or be trained in the use of mechs. Having a daughter who owns a heavy mech filled with advanced technology is a sign of supreme wealth and influence, and a massive mark of pride direct relatives can lord over other noble houses.
