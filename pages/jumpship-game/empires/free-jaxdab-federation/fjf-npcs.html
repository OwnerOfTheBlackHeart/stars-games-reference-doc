Janik Lasker

Janik Lasker is a 7' 5" tall, 8/10 human man with golden blonde hair, ice blue eyes, and enough muscle to box a bear. He normally wears a well fit suit patterned after a nandi bear that does little to hide the man's massive bulk.

Janik serves as the CEO of MEGAcorp and is a member of most of the operating boards of the major subsidiaries, allowing him impressive control over an organization as large and independent as MEGAcorp. Janik commands a small army of wolf-girl super soldiers that are regularly cloned and trained in secret facilities only Janik and those working at them have access to.

Janik Lasker is actually Laine Von Nalbalgrab, a female Maximoffian who brought MEGAcorp to this sector as part of the First Invasion, which failed to get off the ground. Taking advantage of local transtech to become a man, which are expected to run businesses and earn money for their female counterparts, and expand and maintain MEGAcorp. The most valuable thing that Laine is missing in order to begin a proper war-clone program is the base samples.
