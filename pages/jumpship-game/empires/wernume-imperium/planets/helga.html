Helga is the homeworld of the ijudis, despite being somewhat primitive by comparison to the colonies of the ijudis. Thanks to the manipulations of a few individuals, Helga's initial space travel technology was scurried off with once more advanced species made contact with the ijudis, leaving the homeworld deprived of the advanced technology. And thanks to the reputation the ijudis, Helga has been denied a second chance and made to try and catch up with its own colonies, often unsuccessfully.

The planet itself is a comfortable world for life, with excessive amounts of water and swampy terrain spread among the four continents: Mokri, Drvo, Neuspeh, and Nada. Mokri, located on the equator, is the hottest and wettest continent on Helga and the natural territory of the ijudis' ancestors, and also the most populated continent. Drvo is located from the equator to the south pole of the planet and was once both the most populated continent and most heavily industrialize, however local wars have dropped the population heavily and devastated the infrastructure. Neuspeh is the furthest north and the coldest of the continents, which also has the highest percentage of saltwater swamps, has gone from hostile waste to vitally important to Helga as the region is rich with rare earth metals, valuable commodities when trying to advance space travel. Nada is opposite of Mokri on the equator and is the driest of the continents, which makes the ijudis uninterested in developing the inland regions of Nada. MEGAcorp, however, has realized that inland Nada is quite comfortable for human life and have setup an outpost for supporting their trading expeditions into Queel League space, which is little more than a few spaceports and warehouses hidden where the ijudis won't do anything to stop them.

Locations

Mokri

Mokri, located on the equator, is the hottest and wettest continent on Helga and the natural territory of the ijudis' ancestors, and also the most populated continent. The local tribes place low value on the individual and are ready to sacrifice lives to get whatever they want; However, the chieftains themselves are much more cautious, seeing what has happened to Drvo and wishing to protect their tribes' hard to replace holdings from intertribal strife.

Zona

The "capital" of Mokri, Zona is placed almost dead on the equator, leading to it being extremely hot and humid for a temperate world. Little industrial activity actually happens in Zona, with bullet trains carrying locals out of the packed apartments in the center of the city out to the surrounding cities to work at various factories and office buildings in less residential areas. The strong train connections make Zona the clear choice for where to place the primary spaceport of Mokri.

Drvo

Drvo is located from the equator to the south pole of the planet and was once both the most populated continent and most heavily industrialize, however local wars have dropped the population heavily and devastated the infrastructure. The tribes of Drvo have been working to rebuild, but see frequent interference from unknown forces that they believe to be agents of ijudis who benefit from them not having access to jumpships.

Mit

The clear winner of the last major conflict on Drvo, Mit City was able to rebuild its spaceport... and made all the losers pay for it. The city is a mix of seemingly ancient buildings and very recent constructions, though the actual factories are still damaged in ways that are far harder to fix and the population is lower than the local tribe cares to admit.

Neuspeh

Neuspeh is the furthest north and the coldest of the continents, which also has the highest percentage of saltwater swamps, has gone from hostile waste to vitally important to Helga as the region is rich with rare earth metals, valuable commodities when trying to advance space travel. The tribes that control this region are brutal in how they deal with others, happily descending into violence wielding shock clubs and knives so as to avoid damaging anything they actually value. Industrial theft is a constant back and forth in Neuspeh.

Mneto

Using an old strip mine and a few abandoned pit mines as the basis of the spaceport, Mneto is merely the biggest of the options of where to land on Neuspeh and is also technically the safest. Though safest does not mean safe, as the mining conglomerates that command the local tribes are quick to violence when they sense any threat to them or their operations.

Nada

Nada is opposite of Mokri on the equator and is the driest of the continents, which makes the ijudis uninterested in developing the inland regions of Nada. While the costal swamps claim a relatively high population density of ijudis, the inland regions are avoided and considered too inhospitable for permanent habitation. Instead, MEGAcorp has figured out that they can setup an outpost near the center of the continent and use their fast transports to avoid defensive spacecraft in order to have a safe, tax-free haven to serve as a staging ground for operations in the Queel League.

Heaven's Pass

Heaven's Pass is a MEGAcorp outpost operated jointly by STARcorp, SPACEcorp, and TRAVELcorp that serves as a resupply and maintenance stop for spacecraft making the journey to the Queel League and as a vacation resort. Heaven's Pass is made up of a spaceport, an apartment building where the permanent and semi-permanent residents live, a large hydroponics lab, a fancy yet cheap hotel, a suite of amusement rides, and a large surrounding forest treated as a nature park.
