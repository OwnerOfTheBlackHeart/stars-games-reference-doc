Wernume Imperium

  • Primary Species: Kanaka
  • NPCs

The Wernume Imperium, ruled from the Royal Palace on Joantopha, is currently subservient to Emperor Hanalei Kalua's will. All planets within the Imperium serve the Emperor without question, as to do so is the easiest way to maintain their present honor. In turn, the Emperor ensures that all the planets within his realm are protected and well managed, at least well enough that his obligations are fulfilled.

The Wernume Imperium's isolation means that the only neighbors that the Imperium needs to concern itself with are the Queel League, who are a small, non-hostile, and young neighbor, which the Imperium treats like a younger sibling. The ihmisets' love of learning about new locations and history giving the kanaka a chance to brag about themselves and their accomplishments certainly doesn't hurt the relationship.
