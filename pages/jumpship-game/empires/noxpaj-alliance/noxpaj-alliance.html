Nox'paj Alliance

  • Primary Species: Ijudi
  • Primary Currency: Novac
  • NPCs

The Nox'paj Alliance, a structure that only truly exists to serve as a collective defense against external powers that might threaten the ijudi and their territories, serves as the ijudis' federal government. Primarily made of a twice a year moot and a military arm consisting of naval spacecraft and marine forces, the Nox'paj Alliance has minimal, if any, actual influence on non-military affairs requires the federal moot to overcome the personal interests of each planet's chosen chieftains.
