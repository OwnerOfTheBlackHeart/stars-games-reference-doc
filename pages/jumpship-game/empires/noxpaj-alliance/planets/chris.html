Chris is a planet that is only a few steps away from being considered a lifeless rock in terms of livability: An atmosphere made up of inert gasses, a pitiful population that seems to only be on the decline (something they blame on outsiders), no native biosphere to serve as the basis for ensuring environmental ballance, and an equatorial temperature that pushes the limits of what the ijudi are naturally able to tolerate. Still, the locals cling to old laboratories and factories that have been in operation since the ijudi first began colonizing new worlds, using the production of advanced goods to maintain some level of relevance, though the Bolest Plague has been pushing this method of survival to its limits.

The only other thing that Chris has going for it is that the planet has a high water content, which may end up being an inconvenience to other species, but to the ijudi, the mix of moderate to large islands and generally fresh water is a great boon. However, the ijudi keep to three major cities: Veliki Grad, Staro Mesto, and Zaborav.

Locations

Veliki Grad

Veliki Grad is the oldest of the three cities and holds great historic value hidden beneath the layers of modernizations required to keep up to snuff with the other colonies. Made up of a swampy island surrounded by a river and four large, dry islands, Veliki Grad was once a cornerstone of production on Chris, only growing in influence and production as the Bolest Plague depopulated other productive regions and provided both a source of goods and increased needs for the goods produced in Veliki Grad.

Now, however, Veliki Grad has fallen under the destructive wrath of the Bolest Plague and sits on the brink of destruction as a quarantine prevents anyone from leaving and desperation causes violence to become the norm.

Staro Mesto

The youngest of the three remaining cities and holding the largest population, Staro Mesto is made of a dense central city of roughly 200,000 ijudi surrounded by farmland and bogs where the ijudi farm what limited crops and animals they can grow in the inert atmosphere, and providing most of the planet's supply of food.

Zaborav

Zaborav is considered by the locals of Chris to be unworthy of mention, however it is spreading out from Zaborav where most of the planetside mining on Chris is performed. This leaves the city perpetually dirty and an extra miserable place to live for the ijudi sentenced to live here.
