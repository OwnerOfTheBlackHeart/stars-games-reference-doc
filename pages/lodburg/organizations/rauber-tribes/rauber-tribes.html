Rauber Tribes

Description

  • Language: Rauber (Germanic)
  • Values (in order of importance): Relationships, Strength, Honor
  • Primary Entertainment: Animal Racing
  • Common Races: Human (Rauber) and Halfling
  • Name Generator: Generator

The original "discoverers" of Lodburg, which they call Lod, the Rauber were a group of vikings and pirates that established the original forts in Lod to serve as a place to rest and store loot. However, after the Axon forced them inland, the Rauber have begun forming nomadic tribes and forgotten their past as raiders and pirates.

Rauber tribes are lead by a chieftan, the strongest warrior or most respected elder in the tribe, each of which has their own plans and ideas of how they will destroy the Axon and retake their ancestral fortresses.

Raubers do not make much of their own metal, nor do they commonly work the metal they find. Instead, Raubers armor, weapons and tools are most commonly stolen from the Axons. While rare, some brave individuals manage to trade with the Axons, bringing back custom made, and highly coveted, items.

Racial Foci

Spending the last 100 years in the frozen forests and plains of Lodburg, many Rauber bloodlines have been mutated by the snow and fae magics, becoming resistant to their unwilling home.

Level 1: Gain Survive (if male) or Magic (if female) as a bonus skill. Your Strength or Constitution modifier increases by +1 and your Intelligence or Charisma modifier decreases by -1. You are unharmed by mundane extremes of cold and suffer only half damage from magical or extremely intense frost attacks.
