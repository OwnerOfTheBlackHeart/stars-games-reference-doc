The Herta Tribe

Claiming the western side of the Splitmarch to the edge of the Hoarfrost Reach, the Herta tribe is a constant thorn in the side of Direwater, unknowingly exploiting the tension between the Axon and the Wabril.

The current chieftain of the Herta Tribe is Gerlach Herta, a brave warrior who managed to infiltrate Thornhost and commission himself a "noble's" weapon.
