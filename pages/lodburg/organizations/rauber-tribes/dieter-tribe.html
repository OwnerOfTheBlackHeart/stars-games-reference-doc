The Dieter Tribe

Claiming the Squatpoint Mountain and the forests to the north of it, the Dieter Tribe has ample access to all the resources a rauber could need, including fairly weak axons. Using the rivers to quickly move their warriors directly into Axon and the dense forests to cover their retreats, the Dieter Tribe has managed to maintain well trained and well motivated raiders with ease.

The current chieftain of the Dieter Tribe is Kirsa Dieter, a warrior woman who killed the prior chieftain in a duel.

Important Persons

Kirsa Dieter

Kirsa is a 5' 1" tall, female human with flowing, lower-back length black hair, C cup titties, green eyes, and lightly-tanned skin. Kirsa wears a leather vest with a skirt made of bear hide held up by a thick leather belt, heavily furred boots, and red, blood-like paint in stripes across her face and body. She wields a pair of longswords, one she took in a raid on Chillforest and the other from the prior chieftain, as well as a longbow.

Kirsa's policy is one of all out, aggressive war with the Axon, striking at the fortifications quickly before working out and cleansing Lod of the Axon.
