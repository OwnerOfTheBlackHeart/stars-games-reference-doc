The Eike Tribe

Claiming the Gaintfoot Forest and much of the grasslands to the north of it, the Eike Tribe is the single largest Rauber tribe. The Eike Tribe limits its raiding to only when their own supplies are low, letting the Axons get comfortable with the quiet peace, so that they may strike when the Axon least expect it.

The current chieftain of the Eike Tribe is Ivo Eike, a middle aged man who leads his people with care and strategy.

Important Persons

Ivo Eike

Ivo is a 2' 11", male halfling with thick, short brown hair, deep green eyes, and ruddy skin. Ivo is covered in both scars from fighting animals and white tattoos, supposedly granted to him by a faerie. He wears minimal clothes, with only small, scattered pelts and leather belts guarding him against the world. A two-handed axe and a spiked gauntlet make up the weapons that Ivo carries on his person.

Ivo is a masculine herm and has a 3" dick.

Somewhat untraditionally for a man of his age, Ivo gained his position as chieftain through building connections within his tribe and displaying tactical competence. When the prior chieftain died, he elected that Ivo, then a successful hunter of game and Axon scouts, take over as chieftain. To the surprise of the Eike Tribe, none challenged Ivo as he accepted the role of chieftain.

Ivo believes that the way to deal with the Axons would be to launch probing strikes at the various fortresses and launch a full strike on the weakest. Once the fortress is under Rauber control, centralize the tribes and gather enough supplies to support a long term campaign, namely by raiding, and potentially destroying, Axon settlements near the taken fortification. Once the Raubers' position is cemented and secured, working on destroying the remaining settlements and fortifications could commence, though Ivo would not be opposed to using diplomacy from that point to force the Axon to give up their settlements and return to their foreign continents.
