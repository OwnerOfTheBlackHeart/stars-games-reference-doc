The Gunda Tribe

The farthest tribe from the Axon, the Gunda Tribe claims the Hoarfrost Reach as their territory. While normally too far away from the Axon to conduct raids on them, the Gunda Tribe works to prevent Axon colonization of the Reach.

The current chieftain of the Gunda Tribe is Sunnhild Gunda, a youthful shaman with a strong grasp of Rauber politics and social manipulation.

Important Persons

Sunnhild Gunda

Sunnhild is a 5' 7", female human with middle-back length brown hair, frequently with thin, braided strands, C cup titties, hazel eyes, and pale skin with a pinkish undertone. Sunnhild makes up for a lack of battle scars with her shaman paint, the exact shapes and symbols varying from day to day. Sunnhild wears a red, cotton dress taken from the Axon with a cape made from a wolfman and a headband made from a pine twig. As a sign of authority, Sunnhild carries a shaman's staff that is rumored to enhance her magical powers.

Sunnhild came to power by using her status as a shaman to gather a core of loyal supports before convincing the other shamans that having a shaman in charge would be a better way to guide the Gunda tribe on the Rauber path. Through further political maneuvering, Sunnhild was able to find a time where the prior chieftain had only four guards with him, during which she challenged him to a duel with her full force.

Being far from the Axon, Sunnhild recognizes that her tribe has little experience with their capabilities and limitations, and wishes to use probing actions and a careful, measured approach to take down the Axon, as she is certain that none of the other tribes actually understand what the Axon are capable of.

Sunnhild is an elementalist.
