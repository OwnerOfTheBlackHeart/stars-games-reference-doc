The Wabril

Description

  • Language: Yazyk (Russian-based)
  • Values (in order of importance): Wealth, Privacy, Loyalty
  • Primary Entertainment: Song and Dance
  • Common Races: Human, Dragonfolk
  • Name Generator: Generator

A foreign people recently integrated into the Axon after a series of 3, decade long wars ravaged the Georgiy Empire and displaced them across the globe. While Axons are willing to give individual wabril a chance, the Axons view their people, as a whole, as trouble, in no part to the reputation of the Wabril in the Georgiy Empire, as a people neurotically driven to break laws. In an effort to separate themselves from the stigmas, many a wabril will take on axon names.

Common physical traits among the human Wabril include small ears, yellow, orange or red eyes, shiny hair that ranges from white to black and yellow-brown skin. The dragonfolk Wabril commonly have dull scales, long, narrow snouts, thick torsos and are warm to the touch.
