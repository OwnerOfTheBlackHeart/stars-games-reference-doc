The Om'iel Sextant

This strange sextant does not view the sun or the stars, but instead strange, colorful energies that can only be seen through the mirrors of the sextant. A skilled user of the The Om'iel Sextant can use the device to determine what plane they are on, as well as their general position, though this is less accurate than the readings of a normal sextant.
