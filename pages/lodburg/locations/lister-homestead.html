Lister Homestead

Located roughly 20 miles from Direwater, the Lister Homestead is a large, main house surrounded by incomplete supporting buildings, some of which include grain silos, barns, sheds, out buildings and fencing. Much of the construction is, supposedly, delayed by the difficulty of getting resources through the rough hills and the lack of skilled builders on the homestead.

The farmstead mainly grows hearty grains (mainly barley) and sturdy, cold resistant vegetables. The Lister Homestead also hosts a population of goats and longhaired, domestic ox.

The Listers are of the Wabril (pronounced va-bril) people, from the country of Oclana.

The Lister family is secretly slavers who are equal parts breeding slaves and selling slave products, namely magically preserved breast milk, as well as cheese and butter produced from it. They mainly sell to curio traders that port their products overseas to nobles that are willing to pay significant money for such things.

Estrith is always looking for new slaves, especially those that can quickly become "productive." Even better if they produce "special" milk, that has some property only Estrith truly seems to understand.

The Listers took on Axon names upon arriving in Lodburg to help them blend in with the mutt populace.
