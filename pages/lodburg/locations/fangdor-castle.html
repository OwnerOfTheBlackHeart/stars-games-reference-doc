Fangdor Castle

Located on the river's mouth, Fangdor Castle was originally built by Rauber seeking to create a safe place for themselves to hide. However, they were found out and the early Axon took the castle by sneaking in and attacking the Rauber castle from within, as to not damage the castle itself.

The name Fangdor comes from an old iron sign that the settlers found hanging over the door to the keep.
