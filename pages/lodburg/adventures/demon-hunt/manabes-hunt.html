Manabe the Onii

8 19 (impervious defense) +10x2 katana (1d12+4, 6/AC 15) +2 11+ 30ft 10 Unique
  • Onii's Purpose: Any weapon Manabe wields is counted as magic and deals an extra 1d6 to extraplanar creatures.
  • Hunt's Conclusion: As a main action, Manabe can make a single attack that deals 8d6 damage against Manabe's currently assigned target.

A servant of the kami Seki, Manabe is hunting down the demon Chelke to punish her for her crimes against the continent of Uhan. Unfortunately, Chelke is aware of Manabe's presence and has been able to avoid him thus far, utilizing her immunity to the arctic temperatures to her advantage. Now he seeks aid to either kill the demon or to force her into a confrontation with him.

Chelke, Devourer of Galssop

6+6 18a (+1 plate armor) +8 greatsword (1d12+3, 5/AC 15) +3 12+ 30ft 9 Unique
  • Demonic Immunity: Chelke is immune to mundane weapons, and natural extremes of temperature.
  • Elemental Charge: When Chelke is dealt damage by an element (fire, ice, electricity, etc.), a cloud of that element surrounds her, causing all melee or ranged attacks to be rolled twice, using the worse hit and damage.
  • Demonic Regeneration: Chelke heals her hit dice (6) in health at the start of her turn. If she is damaged by holy energies, she loses the effects of Demonic Regeneration for 1 minute.

A demon whose most recent conquests on the continent of Uhan earned her the ire of the kami Seki and her allies among the gods of the Uhan. She is weaker in Lodburg than she's ever been, but this is an equally exciting experience where she can study magic and the manipulation of the material world without the interference of gods. She is also aware of just how easily even a few loyal demons to exert control over the region.

Chelke earned her title as the Devourer of Galssop for single handedly destroying the town of Galssop at the beginning of her attacks on Uhan. The town of Galssop was on the northwestern edge of Uhan, from which Chelke carved a path of destruction to the southeast, until Seki's intervention forcing Chelke to run to Lodburg to avoid retribution at the hand of the gods.
