Weapons

Special Weapon Qualities

Accurate

This weapon is highly accurate and easy to hit targets with, such as a laser. This weapon gains +1 to rolls to hit a target. The pilot can take a special Aim Move Action to focus on aiming to instead gain a +2 to rolls to hit a target. This is the same Aim action as used by weapons with the Ballistic special weapon quality.

Anti-Air

This weapon is optimized for firing at “clean” airborne targets, however, and suffers a -4 penalty to hit when aiming for ground-bound targets.

Anti-Personnel

Armored vehicles, fortifications, and mechs are immune to these light weapons.

Anti-Vehicle

Against armored vehicles and fortifications, roll damage twice and take the higher damage number.

Ammo

The weapon or defense requires ammunition to fire. A number of rounds equal to the weapon’s Ammo rating can be fit into one Ammo Bin fitting. The weapon itself includes one Ammo Bin fitting worth of ammunition as part of the installation. A weapon's ammunition is refilled as part of a normal maintenance cycle.

AP

The weapon ignores this many points of a target’s armor. Reinforcement reduces the effective AP, down to a minimum of 0. It cannot add additional damage to a hit.

Ballistic

This weapon operates by accelerating a solid projectile of appreciable mass to speeds faster than the speed of sound. All ballistic weapons are inherently Ammo weapons. Mechs with the Stability Quadrupedal Mobility System can take a special Aim Move Action to focus on aiming to gain a +1 bonus on rolls to hit a target. This is the same Aim action as used by weapons with the Accurate special weapon quality.

Cone

The weapon fires in a conical pattern that many meters long and wide at its end. All targets within the cone must make an Evasion saving throw or be hit, with no hit roll required. Targets behind hard cover save automatically.

Melee

Melee weapons can only be used in hand-to-hand combat, and no non-melee weapon can be used that same round. Pilots use either Punch or Stab for hit rolls. Mech melee weapons do not inflict Shock damage against mechs with a Reinforcement score higher than the weapon's AP.

Psi

Psi weapons use metadimensional energy channels to completely ignore a target’s Armor rating, except for any armor granted by the MES Shunt Channel defense fitting. Defenses such as Hardened Polyceramic Overlay have no effect on psi weaponry. Pilots may optionally choose to use an appropriate psychic skill with psi weapons, even melee varieties. Psi weapons rely on Wisdom or Constitution to modify attack and damage rolls, rather than Dexterity or Intelligence.

Slow

After firing, the attacker must roll an Int/Shoot skill check against difficulty 8. For psi weapons, use Int and an applicable psychic skill. On a failure, the gun must cool down for one round before it can be fired again.

Unstable

This weapon takes a penalty to rolls to hit targets equal to the speed score the mech is moving at. Mechs with the Stability Quadrupedal Mobility System ignore this special weapon quality.
