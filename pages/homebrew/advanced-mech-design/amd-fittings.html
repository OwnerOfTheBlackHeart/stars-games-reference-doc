Ammo Bin 5k cr 0 1 Light Increase ammunition available to a single weapon Ammo Bin, Armor Piercing 10k cr 0 1 Light Increase armor piercing ammunition available to a single weapon

Ammo Bins provide mechs the ability to carry additional ammunition. For each Ammo Bin, select a weapon on the mech that has an Ammo value. The Ammo bin increases the max Ammo carried by the weapon by the weapon's base max Ammo.

Armor Piercing Ammo Bins work the same as normal Ammo Bins, except that they hold half as much ammo, rounding up, and add 5 AP to the weapon when it is using ammo from the Armor Piercing Ammo Bin.
