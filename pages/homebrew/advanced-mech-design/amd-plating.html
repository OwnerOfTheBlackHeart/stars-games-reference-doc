Plating

Plating describes the armor plating that acts as the skin of the mech, protecting it as much from weapons fire as incidental damage. The primary purpose of plating is to provide the armor score for a mech, but plating also has a reinforcement value, which reduces the Armor Piercing attribute of incoming weapons fire.
