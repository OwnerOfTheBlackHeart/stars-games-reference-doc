Arms

Every mech that intends to manipulate their environment or mount weapons require arms. Arms are distinguished by the weapon mounts, called hardpoints, they support, with the weight of normal arms for the mech being included in the mech's frame. Each arm type has hand hardpoints, that are either held by the mech or mounted onto/in place of the forearm, and shoulder hardpoints that are mounted on, surprise surprise, the arm's shoulder. These hardpoints are denoted by a number followed by a letter with the number being the number of hardpoints and the letter determining the maximum class of the hardpoint(s), L being light, M being medium and H being heavy. The letter N is used in the name to denote no hardpoints.

The location of hardpoint(s) is important in combat. Each hand and shoulder can be targeted independently, but weapons mounted into the same position, such as two light lasers mounted in a LL-M arm's hand hardpoints, are targeted as a group, though they do not all need to be fired at once. Weapons in the same location and of the same type use one attack roll, but separate damage rolls, while weapons of different types in the same location use separate attack and damage rolls.
