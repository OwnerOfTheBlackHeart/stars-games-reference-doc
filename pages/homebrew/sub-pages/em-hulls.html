Improved hulls are mech hulls that are made with TL 5 composite materials, more powerful fusion generators and improved grav plates, in the case of heavy mechs push the boundaries of what even TL 5 can do. Improved hulls are not a hull in themselves, but a modifier to an existing hull, such as an Improved Light Shock hull.

Primitive hulls are mech hulls that are made with substandard components, suffering weaker armor, less durable frames, underpowered fusion generators and wimpy grav plates. While the losses in survivability and capability make most think twice before purchasing primitive mech hulls, those looking for mass produced walking tanks may seriously consider the cost savings of the design.

Primitive hulls are not a hull in themselves, but a modifier to an existing hull, such as an Primitive Light Shock hull.
