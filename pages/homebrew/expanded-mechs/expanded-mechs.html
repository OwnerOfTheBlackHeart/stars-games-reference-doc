The Expanded Mechs system (potentially) adds new hulls, fittings, defenses and weapons to the existing Stars Without Number mech rules, though this document does also include all pre-existing mech hulls, fittings, defenses and weapons for the sake of completeness. The intent it to allow for mechs to feel as if they come from multiple different tech levels.
