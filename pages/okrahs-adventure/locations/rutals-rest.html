Rutal's Rest

A sleepy village in the center of a swamp, Rutal's Rest is home to roughly 200 farms who work the fertile, if not slightly poisonous, lands. Due to its location along the Path of Udona, Rutal's Rest sees semi-frequent traffic from pilgrims.

Important People

Captain Juded Zomod

A tired looking male human wearing a yellow and blue suit of brigandine, Juded serves as the captain of the guard within Rutal's Rest.

He works out of the Guards' Cave.

Heroguard Sorha Yue

A nearly perfect brunette elf with the occasional leaf growing from her hair, she acts as if a grandmother to everyone in Rutal's Rest. While Sorha bakes often, it isn't the act of baking that makes her happy, but watching people enjoy what she's baked.

Sorha serves as the Heroguard for the Shrine of the Rise.

Zinni Jao

The daughter of the local baron, Zinni is a well dressed woman who skirts the line between noble and peasant. She is friendly and loves herself a good celebration.

Notable Locations

The Guards' Cave

Built in an old mine that has been cleaned up and had the lower sections boarded up, the Guards' Cave serves as both the office of Captain Zomod and the village's dungeons.

Shrine of the Risen

A small, rough timber church with a pair of comically large doors dedicated to the Three Above. The inside of the building is kept warm and welcoming by Heroguard Yue. It is common for the church to smell of cookies or fresh bread as Heroguard Yue bakes in the small stone kitchen attached to the back of the Shrine to the Risen.
