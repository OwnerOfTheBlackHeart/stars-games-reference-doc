Okrah's Adventure

  • Location: Rutal's Rest and Arkala
  • Expected Levels: 5

The party starts in Rutal's Rest, the day after Kodia’s Bounty. They start in the Guards' Cave, where Captain Juded Zomod has the party brought in. After the party has done something (leave it vague) during Kodia’s Bounty, Captain Zomod is willing to grant the party full pardon if they help track down Fudar Jame, a young man who went missing during Kodia’s Bounty and a good friend of the baron's daughter Zinni.

Fudar Jame

1 10 +0 unarmed attack (1d2-1) +1 15+ 20' 6 Unique

A young man who's gone missing from Rutal's Rest recently. He's a good friend of Zinni Jao. His most notable feature is an oversized upper-left canine that pokes out when he smiles, though he also has striking silver eyes.

2 15 +2 shortsword (1d6, 2/AC 15) +1 14+ 20' 10 1-4

Wearing armored robes and equipped with a shortsword and small shield, these cult members are active guards of important locations.

Neimar Rusk

4 15 +6 greathammer+2 (1d10+3, 5/AC 18) +2 13+ 20' 11 Unique

The leader of the cult in Arkala, Neimar wields charisma as readily as he wields weapons and spells. Despite not wearing armor, Neimar is difficult to strike, which, combined with his demon gifted hammer, makes him a deadly foe.

Neimar's hammer is named Hyarkmeer and is a greathammer + 2.Hyarkmeer's use does come at a cost, however, as activating the magic hammer costs 1 system strain, which grants the +2 bonus for 24 hours, after which Hyarkmeer must be fed again in order for its magic to function.

Effort: 4

Arts (high mage, pg 67): Sense Magic, Empowered Sorcery, Retain Sorcery, Ward Allies

Spells: Neimar can cast 2 spells per day from the following list: The Coruscating Coffin, Damnation of the Sense, Decree of Lithic Dissolution, Calculation of the Evoked Servitor

Spikeling Demon

2 14 +4 blade arm (1d4+1, 2/AC 13) or
+2 spike throw (1d4) +1 14+ 30' 10 2-6

Spikeling demons are 2 to 3 foot tall, hunchbacked demons with stone gray skin that seems to be constantly shedding and a "cloak" of 6' long spikes on their backs. The arms of a spikeling demon possess blades that appear as if they are made of stone or bone, but are more than capable of punching through metal arm. The heads of spikeling demons are disproportionally small in comparison to the rest of their bodies.

Spikeling demons possess the ability to eject all their spikes at once, creating a field of arrow-like projectiles around them. As a main action, a spikeling demon can deal 2d6 damage to all targets within melee range, no save. After using this ability, the spikeling demon loses access to its spike throw attack for 1 minute as its spikes regrow.

Juphix

6 13 +7x2 slams (2d6, 2/AC 15) or stunning pulse +3 13+ 20' 11 Unique

A 8' 3" tall demon with red-green, sickly looking skin and two tree-trunk like arms, Juphix is the demon to which the demon cult within Arkala hails to. He has several spikeling demons at his disposal.

Juphix has the ability to deploy a stunning pulse, a wave of temporary exhaustion. All foes within 30 feet must make a mental save or lose their main action for their next round. If the target has a holy symbol or similar divine protection, they gain a +4 on the save roll. Juphix can release a stunning pulse in place of one of his normal attacks once every 1d4 turns. 1d4 more turns can be added to the cooldown by striking Juphix with holy water or a holy weapon.
