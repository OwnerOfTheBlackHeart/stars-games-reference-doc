Find the Lab

  • Location: La Contillo
  • Expected Levels: 3-4

Ms. Vivero has a friend by the name of Dr. Gilabert from the Scientific Trust for Understanding the Juice who is in need of some hired muscle.

Dr. Gilabert has managed to identify some old ruins hidden beneath De Dos, that he believes may have been involved in the original development of the Juice. However, his original expedition there revealed that the ruin's defenses are still active, and he requires some extra muscle to make his way inside.

Outline

  1. Artax is asked by Ms. Vivero to help out her friend Dr. Gilabert.
  2. Artax meets with Dr. Gilabert:
    • Dr. Gilabert reveals that he works for, and is a member of, the Scientific Trust for Understanding the Juice
    • Dr. Gilabert reveals that he's found old ruins that he believes to be from before the Scream, which have remained mostly hidden thanks to them being underground and that their security system is still active.
    • Dr. Gilabert believes the ruins to be the tomb of someone important Pre-Scream. He believes that it may be one of the first slavers in De Dos or an important member of the Unitarian Church.
    • Dr. Gilabert needs extra muscle to help break through the ruin's defenses and is willing to 2,000 credits, as well as offer any tools of war that are found within the ruins, as payment.

Enemies

Organizations

The Scientific Trust for Understanding the Juice (STUJ)

The Scientific Trust for Understanding the Juice (STUJ) is a group of scientists dedicated to reverse engineering Juice and furthering their understanding of it. Because of the importance to the Juice to the Unitarian Church, their research is controversial. Members of the clergy that support STUJ argue that by understanding Juice it can be used for other purposes, such as treating genetic diseases, while the opposition argues that the Juice is sacred and property of the Unitarian Church, and that non-clergy should not be allowed to create, modify, own or distribute the Juice.

STUJ is secretly funded and aided by the conesse, who plan to use the research to be able to make their own Juice and Deunification Juice, to allow them to better deploy and retrieve spies.

The League of Separation

The League of Separation is a small rebel movement that seeks to separate church from state.

The League in the Tomb of Saint Dylan Andrade, creator of the Juice, is currently scouting nearby locations for use as staging grounds and training centers. They are hostile a good reason interposes.

Guardians of the Tomb

The Guardians of the Tomb are the seemingly immortal, but not invincible, guardians who swore their loyalty to Saint Dylan Andrade and guard his resting place with absolute zeal. They seek to drive out all the impure from their charge and are immediately violent to anyone they don't recognize.
