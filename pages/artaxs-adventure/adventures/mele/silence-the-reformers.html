Silence the Reformers

  • Location: La Contillo
  • Expected Levels: 3-4

Unifier Leoz is concerned that the Manera Verdadera movement, a group of "noisy" church reformers, has become more aggressive in its teachings lately, openly preaching in the streets and publicly inducting people in mass rituals. He believes Manera Verdadera is towing the line of becoming a distablizing force and that they are distorting the views of the Unitarian Church. He would like them dealt with quietly, whether returned to the orthodoxy or silently eliminated.

Unifier Leoz is offering 10,000 credits, but is flexible in payment value and type, so long as the job can be completed discretely.

Enemies

Organizations

The Manera Verdadera Movement

The Manera Verdadera movement is a religious reformist group within the Unitarian Church that has discovered how to update the Juice to perform a more aggressive transformation, turning all in it into feminine hermaphrodites, which the Manera Verdadera believe is the ultimate for of being Unified.

The Manera Verdadera movement is expanding rapidly at the current moment, and is readily sharing both information on how to make their Juice and encouragement to join in their mass induction ceremonies. A partial cause of the popularity is the fact that their Juice has a chance to make someone more beautiful and/or well endowed, allowing the recipient to reroll all sexual and attractiveness characteristics, taking the higher of their current roll or the reroll. Major monetary donations from Women Again hasn't hurt either.

Zhenshchiny Snova/Women Again

Zhenshchiny Snova, or Women Again, or abbreviated to Zhennova, is a rebel group primarily made up of refugees from Cono who were "forcibly" turned into hermaphrodites, whether immigrants or escaped slaves. Most members of Zhennova wish to be able to be turned back into women and return to Cono, though some of the more zealous members have managed to recruit locals to join Zhennova's cause.

Zhennova, while a destabilizing rebel force that is preparing to actively attack the Unitarian Church, does have some good arguments for their side, namely that Deunification should be more readily available.

Zhennova is primarily supplied by the People’s Republic of Cono, who provides arms, armor, ammo, and training to the rebel group.
