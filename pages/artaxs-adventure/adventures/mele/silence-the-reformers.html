Silence the Reformers

  • Location: La Contillo
  • Expected Levels: 3-4

Unifier Leoz is concerned that the Manera Verdadera movement, a group of "noisy" church reformers, has become more aggressive in its teachings lately, openly preaching in the streets and publicly inducting people in mass rituals. He believes Manera Verdadera is towing the line of becoming a distablizing force and that they are distorting the views of the Unitarian Church. He would like them dealt with quietly, whether returned to the orthodoxy or silently eliminated.

Unifier Leoz is offering 10,000 credits, but is flexible in payment value and type, so long as the job can be completed discretely.

Outline

  1. Oleg is given the adventure by Yuri. If Oleg asks, Yuri will say that he's interested in either selling the machines/parts once refurbished, or keeping them as spare parts for his own shop.
  2. The party travels to the abandoned factory (navigation skill check):
    • If the check is 10, or higher, then the party arrives at the factory after 2 hours of travel.
    • If the check is 8, or higher, then the party arrives at the factory after 3 hours of travel.
    • If the check is 6, or higher, then the party arrives at the factory after 5 hours of travel.
    • If the check is 5, or lower, then the party wastes an hour and must roll again.
  3. The party arrives at the abandoned factory:
    • From the outside, the factory looks like a large, metal dome built at the center of 6 trees. The dome looks sound, though the roof has multiple holes in it. The main entrance to the factory has "Facility 11" written above it in fading letters as well as a half-opened blast door. An appropriate skill check will reveal that the location of the factory was chosen so as to use the trees surrounding it as protection from the hurricane wall.
    • Room 1: The entryway to Facility 11 has only dim lighting and several dead animals with burn marks and large nails sticking out of them. 2 decaying maintenance bots sit at the end of the hallway.
    • Room 2: A small maintenance closet containing various scrapped cleaning machines and empty containers that should hold cleaning chemicals.
    • Room 3: A small maintenance closet containing a waterlogged post-tech toolkit and a small robot/powered armor maintenance stand.
    • Room 4: A small factory floor filled with machines large and small, though only a few are in workable condition. The rest are either waterlogged or have been scavenged for parts. 4 decaying maintenance bots and a refurbished maintenance bot wait in this room.
    • Room 5: A large storage/warehouse area containing water-damaged pallets and empty metal crates. Some of the crates have been disassembled.
    • Room 6: A small hallway guarded by 1 refurbished maintenance bot.
    • Room 7: A stairway leading to a living area. In the living area is a half-ton of rotting foodstuffs, incomplete sets of furniture and some nearly empty bedrooms. Scavenging the area will grant 500 credits.
    • Room 8: Another factory floor area, with many precision manufacturing machines and a clean room containing electronics manufacturing machines, all of which are water-damaged, but operational. 2 refurbished maintenance bots and 2 decaying maintenance bots guard this room.
    • Room 9: A room that holds several server towers as well as transformers and circuit breakers. It is guarded by 2 refurbished maintenance bots.
    • Room 10: A room built around a central fusion reactor, with control units and an AI harness at the back. This room is guarded by 2 refurbished maintenance bots and Gears.
  4. The party returns to Yuri and gets paid.

The Manera Verdadera Movement

The Manera Verdadera movement is a religious reformist group within the Unitarian Church that has discovered how to update the Juice to perform a more aggressive transformation, turning all in it into feminine hermaphrodites, which the Manera Verdadera believe is the ultimate for of being Unified.

The Manera Verdadera movement is expanding rapidly at the current moment, and is readily sharing both information on how to make their Juice and encouragement to join in their mass induction ceremonies. A partial cause of the popularity is the fact that their Juice has a chance to make someone more beautiful and/or well endowed, allowing the recipient to reroll all sexual and attractiveness characteristics, taking the higher of their current roll or the reroll.

Adventure Specific

Decaying Maintenance Bot

1 (3 hp) 9 +0 rivet gun (1d6-1) or +1 welding torch (1d8+1) +1 15+ 15' 12 2-6

A barely functioning industrial maintenance robot, these (physically) strong looking robots have most of their inner parts exposed, though the mounted rivet gun and welding torch give these robots more than enough to maim anyone foolish enough to consider it as harmless.

Refurbished Maintenance Bot

1 (4 hp) 7 +1 rivet gun (1d6) or +2 welding torch (1d8+1) +1 15+ 20' 12 1-4

A fully functioning industrial maintenance bot, these physically strong robots have new, if mismatched, plating and move with a swiftness their uncared for brethren could not hope for. While not made to deflect weapons fire, the plating of this robot offers it minimal protection from intentional harm while its built in rivet gun and welding torch offer it the ability to return the favor.

G-34-R5 (Gears)

2 (10 hp) 4 +4 laser rifle (1d10+1*) or +3 monoblade (1d8+2) +2 14+ 20' 8 Unique

Housed in a blue painted, jury rigged nemesis armature, Gears is the entity in control of "Facility 11," the abandoned factory. 3 years of having to defend and maintain the facility without the return of his masters has caused Gears to become violent.

Gears is a true AI.
