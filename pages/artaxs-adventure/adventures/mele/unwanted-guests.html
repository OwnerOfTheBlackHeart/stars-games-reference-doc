Unwanted Guests

  • Location: La Contillo
  • Expected Levels: 3-4

Unifier Leoz has a small handful of problems that require... digression. This time, a group of offworlders from the other side of the sector, Jimente, are trying to emancipate slaves and convince melians to join in their drug fueled, atheistic utopia.

As the jiments haven't broken any laws, nothing can be done publicly about them, but Unifier Leoz believes them to be a heavily disruptive force that has purposefully chosen to operate in La Contillo to exploit the distraction the pollans' constant military operations create.

Unifier Leoz is offering 10,000 credits, but is flexible in payment value and type, so long as the job can be completed discretely.

Noe Pernet

5 18 unarmored +5 Mag Pistol (2d6+3) +2 13+ 10m 10 Unique

Noe is a 5' 8" tall man with a well kept brown mullet, an average build, a spacefarer's complexion and brown eyes. He wears an outfit not unlike a Warhammer 40k, complete with overly complicated naval uniform, cloak and massive belt buckle. A smile is always plastered across his face.

Noe's objective is to get as many people to take Happy as he can fit into his cargo bays and take back to Jimente to help in the planetary repopulation effort. He also hopes that planting the seeds of emancipation might drive more people to come to Jimente in thanks.

Noe has a 5" dick.

The Emancipator

5 18 assault suit +5 Auto-Mag Carbine (2d6+3*) +1 13+ 10m 12 Unique

What lies underneath the assault suit that makes up the Emancipator is unknown to all but Noe. The suit itself is painted to look as if a walking mural of the sky, with the arms being clouds that move as the Emancipator themself does. In armor, the Emancipator stands at just under 8 feet tall.

The Emancipator is a 6' 8" tall man with a 6" dick.

Jiment Recruiter

2 13 +3 Laser Pistol (1d6) +2 14+ 10m 8 1 plus 2 Jiment Guards

Wearing colorful clothing and a massive smile, this person is ready to extol the values of everyone being free and the benefits of taking the "miracle of joy" that is the drug Happy.

Jiment Guard

Clad in basic security armor and armed with laser SMGs, this cheerful person happily stands guard over what they've been assigned to. Even when pouring fire into their enemies, they can't seem to stop smiling.
