Sister Inez Carderas

Sister Carderas has been receiving reports of strange vehicles being offloaded near a beach that Manera Verdadera uses to import their Juice, as well as other supplies that their location in the abandoned section of La Contillo.

The strange vehicles are actually Conesse technicals and light tanks, supplying and setting up a (currently) mobile military base. The base has 2 technical and 1 Koshka light tank, as well as 30 total soldiers, broken into 3 groups of 9 soldiers and a commissar.
