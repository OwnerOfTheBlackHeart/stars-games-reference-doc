The Empty Eyes/Citreum Kaoutii (key-out-tee)

The Empty Eyes, or Citreum Kaoutii as they would call themselves, are a race of Lost that had arrived on Mele before the Scream. The name Empty Eyes comes from when the first humans who met the kaoutii and believed that their eyes seemed empty and devoid of thought.

Madness

The madness of the kaoutii is unity, the belief that everything must be brought into perfect conjunction and harmony with a greater purpose.

  • Intensity: Strong. The madness directs them in all things, but it does not consume their reason.
  • Motivation: Delusion. A force deceived them into believing that Unity was vital.

Type

The kaoutii are extraterrestrials.

  • Sojourn: Resource extractors for a vanished imperium.
    • Deep sea miners or harvesters of the depths

Shape/Appearance

  • Motif: Material. They have aspects of inanimate material.
  • Format: Human or generally humanoid.
  • Mouthparts: Human-like teeth.
  • Locomotion: Short range teleportation.
  • Integument: Soft skin.
  • Limbs: Digitigrade legs.
  • Vocalizations: Fluting.
  • Smell: Floral.
  • Natural Habitat: Icy tundra.
  • Clothing: Layered robes.
  • Favored Elements: Flowing lines and circles.
  • Unusual Need: Particular emotions (sense of community).

History

  • Obstacle: The world was scourged by some environmental disaster (significant tectonic activity under the ocean).
    • Overcame: Adaptation until the obstacle ceases to be one.
  • Seed of Ruin: Desire to know maddening truths of reality.
    • Worsened Due To: Apathy, an indifference toward resolving it.

Cultural Traits

  • Government: Coded rule by implanted programming.
  • Greeting to Outsiders: Friendly. They welcome outsiders.
  • Favored Virtue: Piety in a faith.
  • Vice: Fleeing from battle.
  • Family Structure: Commercial cabals.
  • Temperament: Deeply paranoid (they want everyone to be friends so they have no enemies).
