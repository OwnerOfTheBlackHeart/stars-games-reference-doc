Mele

Description

  • Atmosphere: Breatheable mix
  • Temperature: Temperate
  • Biosphere: Human-miscible
  • Population: Hundreds of millions of inhabitants
  • Tech Level: TL4
  • World Tags: Misandry/Misogyny and Sectarians
  • Naming Language: Indian (Polla), Russian (Cono), Spanish (De Dos)

The planet Mele is a temperate, Earth-like world with 3 primary continents: Polla, Cono, De Dos. Polla, ruled by the United Counties of Polla, is a highly patriarchal, highly aggressive government driven by conquest and material gains. Cono, ruled by the Peopleâ€™s Republic of Cono, is a highly matriarchal government driven primarily toward internal policy and technological development, even if most of the technology is just for show and corruption runs rampant. De Dos, ruled by the Unitarian Church, is ruled by a government that generally support libertarian ideals with a strong focus on communities supporting each other, which is ruled over by people who willingly undergo genetic modification to become The Unified (hermaphrodites).

A common thread among all three continents is that their ruling governments use religion to justify their existence and to justify the oppression of at least one sex (De Dos is notably less oppressive towards those who refuse Unification). The Unitarian Church, which rules over De Dos, heavily preaches personal freedoms and strength of community, as well as pushing, but not forcing, a ritual known as Unification, in which an individual is genetically modified into a fully functioning hermaphrodite.

Despite the focus on freedom and community service, the Unitarian Church supports a strong slave trade, viewing criminals and captured soldiers as having given up their humanity and became property. Those who become slaves are forced to undergo the Unification, regardless of how willing they are.

Locations

De Dos

Ranging from the norther sub-arctic region to the southern sub-tropical region, De Dos has climates ranging from hot, sandy deserts to taigas to temperate rainforests. De Dos is ruled by the Unitarian Church, and is home to Mele's largest reserves of rare metals.

Vedia

A smallish, flooded city located in the center of Lake El Amor, built around the pyramid-like mansion where the original settlers of Vedia built their homes. From this, a fish farming city sprawls around the city center, which eventually leads to the river Regar, the largest river in central De Dos. At the point where Lake El Amor and the river Regar meet, on the western side of the Lake El Amor, closest to the rising sun, is located the Carcora Temple of the Unitarian Church, which works to ensure the cleanliness of the river Regar by filtering out trash and junk from the delta, then restoring/refurbishing it into salable products.

La Contillo

A fortified city made primarily from reinforced buildings and bunkers, La Contillo is a major military port city for De Dos on the north-eastern shore, and is the closest point to Polla. Due to the military focus of La Contillo, it frequently sees attack by Pollan forces, but just as equally sees templars journey to it in hope of capturing Pollan slaves.
