Clear the Cave

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Shaman Tyreck Mais knows of a small mine that some locals have been looking to recover and use to get some quick and cheap metals, however, several old mernian burrowers have taken up residence in the caves and are preventing the short term mining operation from starting. He's willing to offer 500 credits to have the burrowers cleared out.

Outline

  1. The party gets the job from Tyreck.
  2. The party goes to the mine:
    • If the party walks, it takes 4 hours.
    • If the party rents a ground or hover car, it takes 30 minutes.
  3. The party delves the abandoned mine. Four main chambers and a hallway remain usable, while the rest are flooded:
    1. The entry hallway. Wide enough for a small truck to drive through and connecting the four usable chambers. Holds 2 burrowers waiting in ambush.
    2. First room on the left. A larger, square room. Damaged and turned over furniture suggests a prior use as rest area. 5 burrowers wait in ambush.
    3. First room on the right. Has a door large enough to fit a small truck through. Smashed boxes and a turned over desk suggest an office with a storage area. 3 burrowers wait in ambush.
    4. Second room on the left. Some ruined man-portable mining equipment hints at this room being more exploratory in nature. 4 burrowers lie in ambush.
    5. Third room on the left. Larger, ruined mining equipment is setup in this room, suggesting that it used to be near a vein. 3 burrowers lie in ambush.
  4. The party returns to Tyreck and gets paid.

Enemies

Reusable

  • Old Mernian Burrowers

Adventure Specific

Rewards

Guaranteed

  • 500 credits

Possible

  • 20 tons of scrap metal, worth 50 credits of scrap a ton.
