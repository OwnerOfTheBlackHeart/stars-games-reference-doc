A Small Favor

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Freya Gray wants to give her little buddy Oleg some work. If he comes without Ine, Freya will offer Oleg a job to steal a car. If he comes with Ine, she'll instead ask him to remove of a small street gang, called the Bow (as in the ship term) Street Baggers ("We put bitches in body bags!"). Freya will offer Oleg 2,000 credits either way.

Outline

  1. Party goes to Freya and get's the quest.
  2. Freya describes the target to the party:
    • The car is a souped up ATV explorer with 2 speed, but only 4 armor and 4 crew. It is in one of the Bow Street Baggers garages.
    • The raid location is a small warehouse the Bow Street Baggers use to store and pack drugs.
  3. The car is guarded by the following groups:
    • The two common guards gangers who guard the front door.
    • The four armed common guards in the entry office, 2 of which are armed with monoblades.
    • The four armed common guards in the garage area, 1 with an SMG and 1 with a shotgun.
    • The gravcar that will chase once the car is stole. 4 common guards. 1 driving, the other 3 have combat rifles.
  4. The warehouse is guarded by the following groups:
    • The two common guards gangers who guard the front door.
    • The four common guards in the entry office, 2 of which are armed with monoblades.
    • The six common guards in the main processing area. 2 have stun batons and 2, which are up in the catwalks, that have laser pistols.
    • The four common guards in the storage area, 1 that is armed with a monoblade, 2 with SMGs and a 2 HD guard with a combat shotgun.
  5. Party returns to Freya and gets paid.

Enemies

Reusable

Adventure Specific

Rewards

Guaranteed

  • 2,000 credits

Possible

  • If Oleg gets injured, Freya will give him an extra 1,000 credits to help cover hospital costs.
  • The loot off the dead gangers.
  • Drugs worth 25 credits per encumbrance, though Freya will buy any Oleg brings her for 50 credits a bag.
