Gathering Materials for Something?

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 2-3

Freya Gray would like a small collection of items gathered from the surrounding area. She wants the pelt of a demon orb, the head and neck of a duany's dragon, 2 lites of old mernian burrower blood, as well as 3 eggs, 5 liters of sap from "cherry" tree, 2 growler flowers (just the flower itself), and 2 kilograms of snakeroot roots.

Outline

  1. Party goes to Freya and get's the quest.
  2. Animals: A single search check takes 1 hour.
    • Demon Orb:
      • A demon orb can be found with a DC 9 survival check.
      • The tracks of the demon orb looks like a steam roller went through with occasional "circles" cut into the ground.
      • The demon orb will be found eating the corpse of something that looks like a mix between a deer and a centrosaurus.
      • The flesh itself is being used for making micronuclear devices.
        •
    • Duany's Dragon:
      • A pair of duany's dragons can be found nested down with a DC 8 survival check.
      • The nest is similar to a deer bedding area, nestled under thick brushes and low hanging branches.
      • The heads, to the neck, need to be intact as they are going to be used to extract poison.
        •
    • Old Mernian Burrower:
      • A cave that likely holds old mernian burrower eggs can be found with a DC 9 navigation check or a DC 8 survival check.
      • The cave contains 4 chambers with 1d8-2 old mernian burrowers, with 2d4 eggs appearing in chambers with at least 3 burrowers.
      • The blood is being used to produce a rubber, when mixed with the "cherry" tree sap, while the eggs are going to be processed into nutrient rich MREs.
  3. Plants: A single search check takes 1 hour.
    • The cherry trees can be found with a DC 7 perception check. A single, fully grown "cherry" tree can produce well in excess of 25 liters of sap. A jug of 5 liters of sap is 3 encumbrance and takes 2 hours to fill (due to the pressure required to push the sap up the tree). The sap of the "cherry" trees can be mixed with old mernian burrower blood to produce a rubber or rubber paste.
    • Growler flowers can be found with a DC 9 perception check or a DC 7 survival check. Growler flowers are large, red, yellow and brown flowers with no stems and a central cavity. When disturbed, growler flowers produce a loud, deep growl when disturbed while releasing an oil that produces a meaty smell. Growler flower oil can be used as a meaty perfume and/or flavoring agent that can cover up many common toxins.
    • Snakeroot, a simple and unassuming white flower, can be identified with a DC 7 survival check. A DC 9 survival check reveals that the snakeroot plant is poisonous. 1/2 kilogram of material can be found for each point 7, and above. For example, a roll of 9 would grant 1.5 kilograms. A DC 10 completes the gathering in a single check. Snakeroot can be made into a powdered poison that can be made to look like sugar or salt.
  4. Party returns to Freya and gets paid.
