Duany Eggs for the Arena

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Tremelle Polson is looking for some wild animals to spice up the fights in her arena, specifically some duany's dragons. She asks Sum to gather duany's dragon eggs and offers 1,000 credits for 2 eggs.

Outline

  1. Tremelle gives the party the quest.
  2. The party tracks down duany's dragon nests:
    • Each track attempt takes 1 hour.
    • It is a DC 9 survival to find a nest, DC 7 if the party collects duany's dragon sighting reports.
    • Each nest has 1d3-1 duany's dragons.
    • Each nest has 1d6-2 duany's dragon eggs.
    • Each egg is 1 ENC.
  3. The party returns to Tremelle and gets paid for the eggs they have.

Enemies

Reusable

  • Duany's Dragon

Adventure Specific

Rewards

Guaranteed

  • 1,000 credits

Possible

  • 100 credits for every egg beyond the first 2, to a max of 10 extra eggs
