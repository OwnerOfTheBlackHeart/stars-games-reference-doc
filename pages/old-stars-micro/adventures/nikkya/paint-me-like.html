Paint Me Like...

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Nikkya Calder would like the party help her achieve one of her dreams: To be painted by Sum using paints made from local plants. She'll offer 500 credits to the non-Sum party members for their help. Nikkya will initially eye the party members warily, especially Ine, though she may soften up if they prove they aren't romantic rivals. If they do something that Nikkya perceives as being proof of a romantic rival, she will become violent.

Outline

  1. The party gets the quest from Nikkya.
  2. The party attempts to collect the natural dyes:
    • Golden/yellow dies can be found in Beachtopia's greenhouses/hydrophonics or at the temporary farms.
    • Red dyes can be made from local flowers (DC 7 survival) or from animal blood.
    • White dyes can be made from local tree saps (DC 8 survival) or from demon orb teeth.
    • Brown dyes can be made from local tree bark (DC 8 survival) or from dried and dried duany's dragon venom.
    • Green dyes can be made from local grasses (DC 6 survival) or from old mernian burrower chitin that is crushed and mixed with their blood. A glowing green dye can be made from specially processed demon orb flesh.
  3. Sum paints the painting.
  4. The party returns to Nikkya and gets paid.

Enemies

Reusable

  • Duany's Dragon
  • Old Mernian Burrowers

Adventure Specific

Rewards

Guaranteed

Possible
