Scout the Trackless

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Dispatch Officer Ervan Roan is willing to offer the party a 200 credit bounty for gathering actionable information on the Port-Side Kings. He'll also offer an extra 50 credits per leader the party can bring him actionable intel on, with a 200 credit bounty on the Kings' head, who is currently unknown. If the party spooks the Kings or end up resorting to violence, Ervan will only pay half. If the party asks for one, Ervan can loan them a police issue argus web.

Outline

  1. The party accepts the quest from Ervan.
  2. The party gathers information on the Port-Side Kings' locations:
    • Computer or Bureaucracy DC 8, Culture (criminal) DC 7, Culture (Beachtopia) DC 9. A common place for the Port-Side Kings to hang out at is a bar named the Wingless Whipbird.
    • Computer DC 9, Bureaucracy DC 10, Culture (criminal) DC 8. The Port-Side Kings own a warehouse. DC+2 learns that they use the warehouse drug trafficking and storing stolen goods.
    • Bureaucracy DC 7, Culture (criminal) DC 6, Culture (Beachtopia) DC 8. The Port-Side Kings commonly sell drugs at Rufus Park, which surrounds one of Beachtopia's greenhouses.
  3. The party begins investigating locations:
    • Rufus Park
      • Rufus Park is a hub of Port-Side Kings activity, with multiple dealers hanging around the area.
      • Following a dealer after they finish their daily sales time will lead to the Wingless Whipbird.
      • Investigating the greenhouse reveals that they are growing opium.
    • The Wingless Whipbird
      • The Wingless Whipbird is a fairly popular bar and is filled with drunk and high Port-Side Kings.
      • A locked back room (Security DC 7) holds a common meeting room for the Port-Side Kings. A Perception DC 9 reveals a hidden camera filled with blackmail footage. The footage discusses the warehouse and Jarel.
    • Warehouse
      • Guarded warehouse used by the Port-Side Kings. Persuade DC 8 to convince them that the party is representing a possible customer. Sneak or Athletics DC 9 to sneak in.
      • The warehouse has two main compartments: A main area where the drugs are stored and a garage area where stolen vehicles and goods are stored.
      • A guarded room at the back of the warehouse holds an office for Jarel.
    • Jarel's House
      • This humble house is unguarded, but has semi-frequent traffic.
      • The basement contains 200 kilos of opioids and a small armory worth of bullet vests and light weapons (pistols and SMGs, as well as some rifles).
      • If the party interrogated a well informed ganger earlier, they can wait till Jarel has a meeting with his direct subordinates.

Adventure Specific

Jarel Nunes

3 5 +3 combat rifle (1d12*) +2 14+ 20' 10 Unique

The leader of the Port-Side Kings and cousin of Baron Nunes, Jarel is an imposing, 6' tall boarfolk with a short fuse and an exceptionally large ego. Baron and Jarel don't get along. He normally wears an armored jacket equivalent to a set of woven body armor.
