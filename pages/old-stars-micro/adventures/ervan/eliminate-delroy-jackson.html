Eliminate Delroy Jackson

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Dispatch Officer Ervan Roan is willing to offer the party a 500 credit bounty for eliminating the Delroy Jackson, a local hit man under the employ of the Gravestones. The bounty is doubled if Delroy is brought in alive.

Gravestones identify themselves with 2 gray ribbons worn on their right upper arm.

Outline

  1. The party accepts the quest from Ervan.
  2. The party gathers information on Delroy Jackson:
    • Computer DC 6, Bureaucracy DC 7, Culture (criminal) DC 7. The most common hangout of the Gravestones is the Naked Neon Night Club.
    • Computer DC 8, Culture (criminal) DC 10. Delroy is sleeping with the girlfriend of one of the Gravestones higher up's girlfriends. Beating the DC by 2 grants that the higher up is Malik Mendes.
    • Computer DC 10, Bureaucracy DC 12, Culture (criminal) DC 12. The Gravestones are currently operating 3 safe houses.
    • Computer DC 12. Delroy's next his is a "spy" for Freya Gray.
  3. The party begins investigating locations:
    • Naked Neon Night Club
      • Basic Layout
      • The Naked Neon Night Club is a wide, squat building with painted pink metal siding and what appears to be an office building on the upper floor. The front has a small, red carpet with matching rope barriers in front of a pair of double doors, which have 2 bouncers when the night club is open. The sign is a stylized neon tube removing a skirt and bra.
      • The inside of the NNNC is a typical night club, with a gravitically levitated stage, 2 balcony/loft floors with arena style VIP lounges and an active crowd.
      • Malik Mende and Takira Peate regularly spend time at the Naked Neon Night Club.
    • North 24th Street Safe House
      • A standard, 2 story wooden city home with a relatively new coat of blue paint, this safe house doesn't stand out at all from it's neighbors. Inside the house, however, looks like the result of a wild frat party, with broken and turned over furniture, drug paraphernalia, beer bottles, and food scraps scattered all over the 5 room home.
      • A group of 3 gangers (common guard) are currently staying at the safe house.
    • Taye Drive Safe House
      • From the outside, the Taye Drive safe house looks like a standard small warehouse, with unpainted metal and catwalk windows that could use a little maintenance. The inside, however, includes a 3 car garage, kitchen, bathrooms, multiple bedrooms, a living room and a "commons" room, all fairly nice.
      • This is the safe house that Delroy is staying at. With him are 2 gangers (common guard).
    • Teja Uton Housing Complex Safe House
      • The Teja Uton Housing Complex safe house is a "house" within the Teja Uton Housing Complex, a large, cheap looking wooden building with peeling green paint and 3 stories of "apartments." Each story has 20 "apartments," with each apartment consisting of a hotel style bedroom and bathroom, and a small kitchen.
      • A group of 2 gangers (common guard) are currently staying at the safe house.

Adventure Specific

Delroy Jackson

2 7 +2 SMG (1d8*) or +2 monoblade (1d8+1) +2 14+ 20' 10 Unique

A hit man for the Gravestones, Delroy is a 5' 4" male catfolk with a flat gray coat. He is normally dressed in a lightweight, green peplos, though he frequently changes attire when on the hunt, so as to get close to his enemies. Delroy never goes anywhere without his armored undersuit.

Malik Mendes

3 6 +3 fast-bolt SMG (1d4+1**) or +3 bite (2d6) +2 14+ 20' 11 Unique

The right hand of Takira and effectively second in command of the Gravestones, Malik is a 5' 2" male sharkfolk with sand colored skin with red horizontal stripes and an abnormally vicious mouth, even for a sharkfolk. Malik normally wears an armored evaporative cooling trench coat, using the various water pouches to hide weapons and a dizzying array of recreational drugs, and a pair of basketball shorts with belt loops and pockets sewn on, with a leather belt holding the shorts up.

Takira Peate

4 5 +4 mag pistol (2d6+2) or +4 monoblade (1d8+1) +2 13+ 20' 9 Unique

The leader of the Gravestones, Takira is a 5' 3" female catfolk with an orange and cream tuxedo pattern, D cup titties, and golden eyes. She normally wears a sports bra with a kilt, storing her mag pistol in the kilt pocket. Takira rarely hides her monoblade, which looks like a typical hunting knife that she keeps in a sheath attached to her bra, under her left armpit.

Despite not wearing any armor, Takira has mild psionic abilities that give her an effect similar to wearing a set of woven body armor.
