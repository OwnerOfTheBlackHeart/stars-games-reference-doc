Delroy Jackson

2 7 +2 SMG (1d8*) or +2 monoblade (1d8+1) +2 14+ 20' 10 Unique

A hit man for the Gravestones, Delroy is a 5' 4" male catfolk with a flat gray coat. He is normally dressed in a lightweight, green peplos, though he frequently changes attire when on the hunt, so as to get close to his enemies. Delroy never goes anywhere without his armored undersuit.

The leader of the Port-Side Kings and cousin of Baron Nunes, Jarel is an imposing, 6' tall boarfolk with a short fuse and an exceptionally large ego. Baron and Jarel don't get along. He normally wears an armored jacket equivalent to a set of woven body armor.
