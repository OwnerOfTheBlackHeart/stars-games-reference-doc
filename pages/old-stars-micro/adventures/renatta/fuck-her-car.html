Fuck Her Car

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 2-3

Renatta Gayle has a particularly annoying romantic rival, however Renatta can't just have her killed, namely because her dad threatened her if she did. Instead, Renatta would like to have her rival, Courtney Hitchman's car to be wrecked.

Renatta considers Jorell to be her man, with Courtney being some harlot trying to take Jorell away from her while he's in Beachtopia.

Outline

  1. Renatta offers the party the job and is very explicit that no harm is to come to Courtney herself. Renatta will also give the party Courtney's address.
  2. The party travels to Courtney's house:
    • The house itself is a 3 story, artisan wood mansion with plentiful stained glass windows, a large backyard with a pool, gardens in the front and a 5 car garage. The mansion is built on the prow of Beachtopia.
    • In the garage are the following vehicles:
      • A classically styled, methanol fueled motorcycle.
      • A grav-limo (grav car with 10 crew, but only 1 speed).
      • A hot pink grav sports car (3 speed, 2 crew).
      • An ATV explorer.
      • A microlight flyer (Engines of Babylon, pg. 5) on a trailer.
  3. The party returns to Renatta and gets paid.

Adventure Specific

Courtney Hitchman

1 7 +1 pistol (1d6+1) +1 15+ 20' 6 Unique

Courtney is a 5' 6" foxfolk woman who Renatta considers a romantic rival. She competes with Renatta for Jorell's affection. Courtney is unaware of Renatta's or Jorell's connection to the Darkwire Gang.

Courtney normally wears sequined dresses and fitted shirts, usually showing off her slim figure.

Jorell Duff

1 6 +3 laser pistol (1d6+1) +1 15+ 20' 9 Unique

Jorell is a 6' otterfolk man who is the romantic interest of Renatta and Courtney. Jorell is a member of the Darkwire Gang who is located in Beachtopia as a weapons seller.

Despite being 6' foot tall, Jorell manages to maintain a lithe figure and boyish looks. He typically wears the synthflesh over his metal left arm, as well as a pair of tight jeans and, occasionally, a nice t-shirt, though sometimes he wears a nice, well fitted suit.
