Parts for Darkwire

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Engineer Yuri has some custom machine work for Old Burt that he needs delivered, but he needs it done by someone he can trust to get certain parts to Darius. Yuri will offer an armored undersuit for this job. If told of Oleg's broken still, he'll also offer Oleg a day's worth of workshop time, and some left-over pipes and scrap metal.

If Oleg accepts, Yuri will inform him that he should wear his "colors" (Fellowship of Steel uniform) so that Darius knows who sent him.

Outline

  1. Oleg is given the adventure by Yuri.
  2. Yuri gives Oleg 3 crates to deliver:
    • A crate as large as a person. ENC 6. This crate contains a mix of laser and cutter rifles. Yuri warns these will be counted on arrival. This crate is for Baron Nunes.
    • A crate as large as a human torso. ENC 4. This crate contains black, cybernetic left arms with synth skins, each of which has a tag on the middle finger. This crate is for Lieutenant Kate Snee.
    • A crate half the size of a human torso. ENC 3. This crate has tamper detection systems and is clearly more damage resistant than the other 2 crates. Yuri says that this crate is to be delivered to Darius directly and that it should not be opened under any circumstance. This crate contains an air to water life support conversion kit for powered armor.
  3. The party goes to Old Burt:
    • If the party decides to walk, it takes 12 hours to make the journey and they will encounter a duany's dragon.
    • If the party rents a ground jeep, it takes 4 hours and they don't have any encounters.
    • If the party rents a grav car, it takes 3 hours and they don't have any encounters.
  4. The party arrives and begins hunting down the delivery targets:
    • Baron Nunes can be found at The Baron's Armory.
    • Lieutenant Kate Snee has a 50/50 chance be found at Old Burt's hospital or at the training grounds with the group of uncybered recruits the arms are for.
    • Darius can be found at the Ethanol Cylinder's basement arena. Entry to the arena requires either a DC 8 persuade check, an entry for the arena fight (50/50 flesh or bot), or Oleg saying he has a package for Darius and refusing to let the door guard deliver it 3 times. The last option is only available if Oleg is in his Fellowship of Steel uniform.
  5. The party returns to Yuri and gets paid.
