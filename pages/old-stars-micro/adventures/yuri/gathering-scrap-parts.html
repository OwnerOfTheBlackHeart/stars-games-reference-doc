Gathering Scrap Parts

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 1-2

Engineer Yuri has discovered an abandoned factory full of damaged and waterlogged machines a ways away from Beachtopia. Yuri wants the machines within the factory so that he can repair/refurbish them. Yuri will offer a suit of woven body armor, a laser rifle and a bundle of 3 A cells for this job.

Due to the size of the machines involved, Yuri will offer the party the use of 2 of his delivery trucks and 4 workers trained in operating the removal equipment.

Outline

  1. Oleg is given the adventure by Yuri. If Oleg asks, Yuri will say that he's interested in either selling the machines/parts once refurbished, or keeping them as spare parts for his own shop.
  3. The party returns to Yuri and gets paid.

Enemies

Reusable

Adventure Specific

None.

Rewards

Possible
