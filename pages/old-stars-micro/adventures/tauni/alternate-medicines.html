Alternate Medicines

  • Location: Beachtopia
  • Expected Levels: 2-3

Staff Sergeant Tauni Ripley is concerned because the Beachtopia Rapid Combat Force (RCF) is having trouble getting medical supplies. After some research, Tauni has identified several alternative medicines of interest, and would like the party to acquire enough to tide the RCF over until they can deal with their medicine issues.

Outline

  1. Tauni gives the party the quest.
  2. Tauni needs the following materials to make her medicines:
    • 4 liters of duany's dragon poison.
    • 1 liter of old mernian burrower blood.
    • 5 bundles of zeazeac darts.
    Tauni wants the different items because:
    • Duany's dragon poison is useful as a local anesthetic.
    • Old mernian burrower blood can be mixed with common food items to make an artificial scab gel.
    • Zeazeac quills are coated in a disinfectant that can be extracted if the quills are stored quick enough.
  3. The party returns to Tauni and gets paid.

Adventure Specific

Zeazeac

1 7 +2 bone dart (1d4) +1 15+ 20' 8 4-8

The zeazeac is a meter tall, gangly looking quadrupedal mammal with mottled brown, green and yellow fur, a long, narrow muzzle, tall, wide ears that lay flat along its neck when threatened and bright orange, forward facing eyes. Inside the mouth of the zeazeac is a small organ that allows the zeazeac to release a small, barbed dart made of bone into its mouth, which the zeazeac launches using its tongue and a hard section of the roof of its mouth. On the backs of the legs of the zeazeac is a climbing claw that allows the zeazeac to have surprisingly nimble arboreal movement.

Zeazeacs move in packs of 4 to 8, though on rare occasions groups as small as mating pairs may travel about or packs as large as 19 can form. Most of the time, zeazeacs only descend from the trees to move about or feast on freshly killed prey. Despite their prevalence among the jungles surrounding Beachtopia, zeazeacs are rarely a problem for humans and anthromorphs, as the zeazeacs don't normally attack tall prey.
