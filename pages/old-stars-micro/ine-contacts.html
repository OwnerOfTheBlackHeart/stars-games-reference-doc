Ine's Contacts

Shaman Tyreck Mais

One of the local shamans of Tenrikyo, Shaman Mais typically handles Ine's watch of templars. As per his position, Shaman Mais keeps careful watch over the movements of local animals and predators (in particular), nearby pirates and raiders, violent religious sects, and whatever else might be a threat to Beachtopia's flock.

Tyreck is a 6' 5" tall, male catfolk with brown and gray, tabby patterned fur, long, wide and untufted ears and green eyes. Tyreck normally wears a set of lightweight, green and blue, silken robes with several pendants depicting the various gods of Tenrikyo. Underneath the robes is a loincloth with either boots or sandals, depending on the terrain he is expecting to be on that day.

Dispatch Officer Ervan Roan

A dispatch officer for Beachtopia's police force, Ervan normally handles templar and SWAT dispatch. While normally waiting around for the next big event too difficult for normal police to handle, Ervan gathers reports that the police either can't act on (for legal reasons or because they'll spook someone they can't yet arrest) or that are outside normal police jurisdiction, offering these reports to templars in need of busy work.

Ervan is a 5' 10" tall, male human with tanned skin, short, spiked brown hair and brown eyes with an orange tint. When on duty, Ervan wears the standard dark gray with bright yellow stripes uniform of the Beachtopia police force with a thermal pistol and a stun baton on his hips. When off duty, Ervan will normally wear a light t-shirt and a pair of shorts (type varies by what he's planning on doing) with sandals or hiking shoes.
