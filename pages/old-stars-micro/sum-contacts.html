Tremelle Polson

Tremelle is the owner of Polson Housing and Transportation (PHT), which builds embarkable homes and 1 to 4 seat all terrain vehicles. Due to the popularity of PHT's products, and frequent contracting during new city construction, Tremelle is quite wealthy for a resident of Beachtopia. Tremelle doesn't delve much into crime, though she owns the Ripcord and Rum, a bar that is popular with fixers and has Tremelle's beloved fighting pit.

Tremelle is a 5' 3" tall, pudgy otter girl with B cup titties, well cared for chocolate brown fur and brown eyes. Tremelle typically wears layers of colored silks and other thin, breathable materials over a well made black dress and plentiful jewelry. While she dresses the way she dresses to look wealthy, Tremelle puts in the time to make sure that all her garments and jewelry go together.

As an expression of wealth, Tremelle keeps a rabbit girl (Jasmine East) and a bat girl (Raca McCullock) with her at all times. When at the Ripcord and Rum, Tremelle will sit on a throne overlooking the fighting pit (and whole room) with Jasmine and Raca on either side, leaning up against the padded sides of the throne.
