Oleg's Contacts

Engineer Yuri

An engineer from the Fellowship of Steel chapter on Sovietallia, Yuri is one of the few beacons of familiarity to Oleg. Yuri primarily operates out of Not Kitten Manufacturing, a custom order manufacturing shot that builds everything from parts for home appliances to small order military equipment.

Yuri is a 6' 3" human male with a, nearly blonde, light brown high and tight with slightly tanned skin and hazel eyes. He usually wears his brown and green engineer's jumpsuit, laden down with tools and parts. When caught outside of Not Kitten Manufacturing, Yuri may wear a pair of cargo shorts with a light, hawaiian shirt that is kept mostly buttoned and tucked in.

Not Kitten Manufacturing

Named after a pun from the old Sovietallian game Monster Slayer, this shop looks like a normal small machine shop from the outside, with a simple sign, blue painted metal walls, and unassuming glass entry doors, inside the actual manufacturing area of Not Kitten Manufacturing is filled with highly advanced manufacturing equipment and even an overhead crane. Not Kitten Manufacturing doesn't look large from the front, though the building itself is quite deep.

Freya Gray

While considered a great bane by Oleg, Freya likes to fancy herself as a friend of Oleg, despite how much Oleg mistreats her. Though, one might think that she does so BECAUSE Oleg resists her. Unfortunately for Oleg, Freya always seems to have something for Oleg to do, as well as the money to compensate Oleg for their services.

Freya is a 5' 5", calico patterned female catfolk with E (DD) cup titties, short, untufted ears, and heterochromatic eyes, with the left eye being yellow and amber and the right eye being blue/orange dichroic. Freya normally wears little more than a bikini (several different sets) with thigh high boots, though she always has at least a mag pistol and a monoblade xiphos on her person.

Freya has connections with several groups within Beachtopia, both legal and illegal, usually serving as an info broker and a fence.
